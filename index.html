Biography

I am a final-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, ETH Zürich. I am fortunate to be supervised by Rasmus Kyng. My research interests lie in algorithms and optimization, with a specific focus on fast algorithms and complexity analysis for Linear Equations and Linear Programming.

Before coming to ETH Zürich, I received my Bachelor’s degree in 2017 from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). Additionally, I participated in a dual-degree program, obtaining a Diplôme d’Ingénieur from CentraleSupélec and a Master’s degree from SJTU in 2020, where I worked with Prof. Xudong Wang.

In Fall 2023, I visited the Simons Institute at UC Berkeley for the program “Data Structures and Optimization for Fast Algorithms”.
